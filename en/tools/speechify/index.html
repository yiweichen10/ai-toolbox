What is Speechify?

Speechify is an AI-powered text-to-speech platform that transforms written content into natural-sounding audio. Designed for students, professionals, and individuals with reading challenges like dyslexia, it converts textbooks, documents, emails, and web articles into high-quality speech. Available as a web app and mobile application, Speechify helps users consume content 2-3x faster than traditional reading.

Key Features

  • Natural AI Voices: Choose from 100+ human-like voices across genders and accents, including celebrity voices like Snoop Dogg and Gwyneth Paltrow in premium plans.
  • Multi-Language Support: Convert text in 100+ languages including English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Hindi with accurate pronunciation.
  • Speed Control: Adjust playback from 0.5x to 4x speed (up to 900 WPM for power users) without pitch distortion.
  • OCR Scanning: Take photos of physical books or documents to instantly convert printed text to audio using optical character recognition.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Start listening on your computer and continue on your mobile device with automatic progress saving.
  • Dyslexia Tools: Special features like text highlighting, font adjustments, and color overlays to enhance readability.

Pricing

Speechify offers three pricing tiers:

  • Free Plan: Basic voices at normal speed (10 voices max), 20 minutes of OCR scanning per month
  • Premium ($10/month billed annually): All premium voices, unlimited listening speeds, unlimited OCR, note-taking features
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for schools and businesses with team management and advanced analytics

Compared to alternatives like NaturalReader or Murf.ai, Speechify provides better value for educational users with its specialized dyslexia support and celebrity voice options.

Pros & Cons

Advantages: Speechify stands out for its exceptionally natural AI voices, robust mobile functionality, and features specifically designed for learning disabilities. The OCR scanning capability makes it versatile for both digital and physical text conversion.

Limitations: The free version is quite restricted, and some users report occasional mispronunciations with technical terms or non-English words. The mobile app consumes significant battery life during extended use.

Speechify vs Alternatives

  • vs NaturalReader: Speechify offers more natural-sounding voices and better mobile integration, while NaturalReader has stronger PDF annotation features.
  • vs Amazon Polly: Speechify provides simpler user interface and consumer-focused features, whereas Polly is more developer-oriented with deeper customization.
  • vs Murf.ai: Speechify specializes in reading assistance, while Murf focuses more on voiceovers for video content creation.

Speechify's edge lies in its educational focus, with features like variable speed control and text highlighting that competitors often lack.

Best For

Speechify is ideal for:

  • Students with dyslexia or reading difficulties
  • Professionals who want to consume reports and emails hands-free
  • Language learners improving pronunciation and listening skills
  • Commuters converting articles or books to audio
  • Visually impaired users needing text conversion
  • Researchers digesting large volumes of academic papers

Verdict

Speechify is one of the most polished text-to-speech solutions available, particularly for educational and accessibility purposes. While the premium pricing may deter casual users, the quality of voices and specialized features make it worthwhile for students, professionals, and anyone who consumes large amounts of text content.
