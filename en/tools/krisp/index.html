What is Krisp?

Krisp is an AI-powered noise cancellation app that removes background sounds from your calls and recordings in real time. Designed for remote workers, content creators, and business professionals, it transforms any microphone into a studio-quality input by eliminating distracting noises. Unlike hardware solutions, Krisp works with standard headphones and doesn't require expensive equipment.

Key Features

  • AI Noise Cancellation: Removes over 300 types of background noises including keyboard clicks, barking dogs, and street sounds while preserving voice clarity
  • Echo Cancellation: Eliminates room echo and reverb for professional-grade audio during video conferences
  • Multi-Platform Support: Works seamlessly with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Discord, and 800+ other communication apps
  • Virtual Devices: Creates virtual microphones and speakers to filter audio across all applications without changing hardware settings
  • Meeting Transcription: AI-powered notes automatically transcribe meetings with speaker identification (Pro plan)
  • Lightweight Operation: Uses minimal CPU resources (under 5%) without slowing down your computer

Pricing

Krisp offers a freemium model:

  • Free Plan: 90 minutes of noise cancellation per week with basic features
  • Pro Plan: $12/month (billed annually) or $16/month (monthly) for unlimited usage, meeting transcription, and HD voice quality
  • Teams Plan: Custom pricing for businesses with centralized billing and admin controls

Compared to hardware noise-canceling solutions that cost hundreds of dollars, Krisp provides exceptional value with its software-only approach.

Pros & Cons

Krisp excels at delivering studio-quality audio without expensive equipment, working across all communication platforms with impressive AI accuracy. The free version serves as an excellent trial, though power users will need the Pro plan. Limitations include no Linux support and occasional over-filtering of subtle voice nuances.

Krisp vs Alternatives

  • Krisp vs NVIDIA RTX Voice: While both use AI noise cancellation, Krisp supports more platforms (including Mac and mobile) and offers better battery efficiency on laptops
  • Krisp vs Dolby Voice: Krisp requires no specialized hardware and provides more consistent noise removal across different microphone qualities
  • Krisp vs Sonar: Krisp processes audio locally (better for privacy) versus Sonar's cloud processing which adds latency

Krisp stands out for its plug-and-play simplicity, exceptional background noise suppression, and cross-platform compatibility.

Best For

  • Remote workers taking calls in noisy environments
  • Podcasters & streamers needing studio-quality audio without expensive gear
  • Customer support teams requiring clear communication with clients
  • Online educators recording lessons without background distractions
  • Hybrid teams conducting professional meetings from various locations

Verdict

Krisp is one of the most effective AI noise cancellation tools available, transforming any headset into a professional-grade microphone. With its affordable pricing and seamless integration, it's a must-try for anyone making important calls or recordings in less-than-ideal acoustic environments.
