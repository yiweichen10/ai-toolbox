What is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription service designed for professionals, students, and teams who need accurate meeting notes. This smart assistant records audio, transcribes conversations in real-time, and generates organized summaries. Ideal for remote workers, journalists, and students, Otter.ai helps users focus on conversations while automatically capturing every word.

Key Features

  • Real-time Transcription: Instantly converts speech to text during live meetings, lectures, or interviews with impressive accuracy
  • Speaker Identification: Automatically detects and labels different speakers in group conversations
  • Smart Search: Find specific moments in recordings using keyword search within transcripts
  • Collaboration Tools: Share transcripts with team members, add comments, and highlight important sections
  • Integration: Works seamlessly with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Dropbox
  • Mobile Accessibility: iOS and Android apps allow recording and transcription on-the-go

Pricing

Otter.ai offers a generous free plan with 300 monthly transcription minutes (30 minutes per session). Paid plans include:

  • Pro ($16.99/month): 1,200 minutes monthly, advanced export options, and custom vocabulary
  • Business ($30/user/month): Team features, admin controls, and centralized billing
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions with advanced security and support

The free version makes Otter.ai one of the best free AI tools for students and individual professionals needing basic transcription.

Pros & Cons

Otter.ai stands out for its real-time transcription accuracy and excellent free tier, making it accessible for personal use. However, the session limits on free accounts and occasional transcription errors with specialized vocabulary may require manual corrections. The mobile experience is particularly strong compared to alternatives.

Otter.ai vs Alternatives

Compared to Rev.com, Otter.ai offers free transcription and real-time capabilities, while Rev focuses on human-verified transcripts. Against Sonix.ai, Otter provides better mobile functionality but lacks Sonix's advanced translation features. Fireflies.ai offers similar meeting assistance but Otter's cleaner interface and free plan make it more accessible for casual users.

Best For

  • Remote teams conducting frequent video meetings
  • Journalists needing interview transcriptions
  • Students recording lectures and study sessions
  • Professionals who attend multiple meetings daily
  • Content creators repurposing audio into written content

Verdict

Otter.ai is one of the best free AI transcription tools available, especially for real-time meeting notes. While perfect accuracy isn't guaranteed, its convenience and collaboration features make it worth trying for anyone who regularly needs conversation transcripts. The free plan provides substantial value before committing to paid options.
