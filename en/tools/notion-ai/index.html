Notion AI Review 2026: Best AI for Knowledge Workers?

Notion AI removes the friction of switching between your notes app and an AI assistant. For teams already using Notion as their knowledge base, it's the most contextually aware option available.

Key Use Cases

  • Summarizing long meeting notes into action items
  • Drafting project proposals from bullet points
  • Translating documents for international teams
  • Auto-filling database fields with AI-generated content

Pricing

  • AI Add-on: $10/user/month added to any Notion plan
  • Notion Free + AI: $10/month total for solo users
  • Notion Plus + AI: $18/month combined

Verdict

If you live in Notion, the AI add-on is worth it for the convenience alone. If you're not a Notion user, it's not worth switching just for the AI features — use ChatGPT or Claude instead.

👍 Pros

  • Native integration with all Notion content
  • No context switching between tools
  • Good at summarizing meeting notes
  • Supports 30+ languages

👎 Cons

  • Requires Notion subscription plus AI add-on
  • Less powerful than standalone AI tools
  • Cannot access external data

❓ FAQ

Do I need a paid Notion plan to use Notion AI?

Notion AI is available as an add-on for $10/month on top of any Notion plan, including the free plan.

Can Notion AI search my entire workspace?

Yes, the Q&A feature can search and synthesize information across all pages you have access to in your workspace.
