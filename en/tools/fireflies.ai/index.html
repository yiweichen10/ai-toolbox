What is Fireflies.ai?

Fireflies.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant that automatically joins, records, transcribes, and analyzes your virtual meetings. Designed for professionals and teams, it works seamlessly with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and other platforms to create searchable transcripts, highlight key moments, and extract action items. Ideal for remote workers, managers, and anyone who wants to eliminate manual note-taking.

Key Features

  • Smart Meeting Transcription: Automatically converts meeting audio to text with timestamps and speaker identification in 60+ languages
  • AI Summaries: Generates concise meeting summaries with key points, decisions, and questions using natural language processing
  • Action Item Tracking: Identifies and extracts tasks, deadlines, and commitments mentioned during discussions
  • Knowledge Search: Search across all past meetings by keyword, topic, or participant with Google-like search functionality
  • Integrations: Works with CRM tools like Salesforce, project management apps like Asana, and storage platforms like Dropbox

Pricing

Fireflies.ai offers a generous free plan with 800 minutes of storage (transcription credits) per month. Paid plans start at $10/user/month (Pro plan) with unlimited transcription, advanced analytics, and CRM integrations. Enterprise plans include custom vocabulary training and priority support. Compared to alternatives like Otter.ai, Fireflies provides better free tier functionality for casual users.

Pros & Cons

Pros: Saves hours on meeting documentation, creates shareable records for absent colleagues, reduces miscommunication through accurate transcripts, and offers useful free tier. Cons: Speaker identification can require manual correction, real-time transcription lags slightly behind live conversation, and export formatting options are limited.

Fireflies.ai vs Alternatives

Compared to Otter.ai, Fireflies offers better integration with business tools and more comprehensive free tier features. Versus Gong.io (which focuses on sales teams), Fireflies is more affordable and suitable for general business use. Unlike Zoom's native transcription, Fireflies provides searchable archives across multiple platforms and creates structured meeting summaries with actionable insights.

Best For

  • Remote teams needing centralized meeting records
  • Project managers tracking action items across multiple discussions
  • HR professionals documenting interviews and performance reviews
  • Consultants who bill clients based on meeting time
  • Students recording lectures and study group sessions

Verdict

Fireflies.ai is one of the most practical free AI tools for meeting productivity. Its automatic summaries and action item extraction provide immediate value, while the searchable knowledge base becomes indispensable over time. Highly recommended for anyone attending more than 5 meetings per week.
