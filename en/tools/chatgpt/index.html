ChatGPT Review 2026: Is It Still the Best AI Assistant?

ChatGPT remains the most widely-used AI assistant in the world, with over 100 million weekly active users. Since the release of GPT-4o, the free tier now includes multimodal capabilities that were previously locked behind the Plus subscription.

Who Should Use ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is ideal for writers, developers, researchers, and business professionals who need a general-purpose AI assistant. Its Custom GPT marketplace offers thousands of specialized tools built on top of the base model.

Pricing Breakdown

  • Free: GPT-4o with usage limits, image generation, voice mode
  • Plus ($20/month): Higher limits, GPT-4o priority, advanced tools
  • Team ($25/user/month): Shared workspace, admin controls
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing, SSO, compliance features

Verdict

ChatGPT is the safest default choice for most users. Its combination of capability, ecosystem, and free tier makes it unbeatable for general-purpose AI work.

👍 Pros

  • Best-in-class general reasoning
  • Huge plugin and GPT ecosystem
  • Strong code generation and debugging
  • Free tier available with GPT-4o

👎 Cons

  • Can hallucinate facts confidently
  • Knowledge cutoff limitations
  • Plus plan required for latest models

❓ FAQ

Is ChatGPT free to use?

Yes, ChatGPT offers a free tier with access to GPT-4o (with limits). The Plus plan at $20/month unlocks higher rate limits and priority access.

What can ChatGPT do that other AI tools can't?

ChatGPT has the largest ecosystem of third-party plugins and Custom GPTs, plus strong multimodal capabilities including image understanding and generation via DALL-E.
